Because, let's face it. Each language is suited to a specific set of needs and sometimes it's too much hassle to code 'close to the machine' as it were, using a high-level language.

The Problem Solver

Ah yes, the bread and butter of my existence. The reason why I lay my fingers across my keyboard and type curly braces and jargon that makes no sense in an English sentence.

The thing that I enjoy most about software development is that it presents you with problems. Most of the time, these problems stem from previously written algorithms that we never account for. I love these problems because it is what makes perfect code perfect...In the end. And, it's a driving force, it's why I shoot up out of bed some nights; because I've thought of the solution.
